Banana bread

No need for a mixer for this recipe!
Clean-up is easy too, if you want, you can mix everything in one mixing bowl.
The best bananas to use for banana bread are those that are over-ripe.
The yellow peels should be at least half browned, and the bananas inside squishy and browning.
https://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/banana_bread/

List of Ingredients:

  • 2 to 3 very ripe bananas, peeled
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar (1/2 cup if you would like it less sweet, 1 cup if more sweet)
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C), and butter a 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, smash the riped bananas with a fork until completely smooth.
    Stir the melted butter into the smashed bananas
  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt. Stir in the sugar,
    beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix in the flour.
  4. Pour the batter into your prepared loaf pan.
    Bake for 50 minutes to 1 hour at 350°F (175°C), or until a tester inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Remove from oven and let cool in the pan for a few minutes.
    then remove the banana bread from the pan and let cool completely before serving slice and serve.
    (A bread knife helps to make slices that arent crumbly.)

Tiramisu

A little taste of italian heaven paired with your favorite COFFEE MATE treat.
https://www.madewithnestle.ca/recipe/coffee-mate-caf%C3%A9-mocha-tiramisu?gclid=CjwKCAjwmJbeBRBCEiwAAY4VVSqZ2EsJ93aXMiWYjFSJi35PwqmmBGzEHAuiximlwNkGllYj8QN1-xoC7V8QAvD_BwE&gclsrc=aw.ds&dclid=CMeI-6uri94CFRDDwAod0sQAFQ

List of Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup Coffee mate cafe mocha liquid
  • 3 tbsp Nescafe tasters choice instant coffee
  • 1 container (8 oz) mascarpone cheese
  • 1 container (8 oz) frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 12/3 cup hot water
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur or brandy (optional)
  • 32 dry ladyfinger biscuits (approx. 27-oz packages)
  • Cocoa powder

Steps:

  1. Combine mascarpone cheese and COFFEE-MATE Café Mocha in large bowl; stir until smooth. Fold in whipped topping.
  2. In another bowl, stir NESCAFÉ Taster’s Choice Instant Coffee in hot water until dissolved. Stir in liqueur if desired.
  3. Dip half of the ladyfingers (one at a time) into the coffee mixture (no need to soak, just a quick dip) and line the bottom of a 9 inch square baking dish. Top with half of the whipped topping mixture. Dip and layer the remaining ladyfingers. Top with remaining whipped topping. Dust top with cocoa powder.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving. Can be stored, covered, in refrigerator for up to 3 days.
